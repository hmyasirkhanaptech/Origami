How to Fold an Easy Origami Cat Face

These instructions will show you how to fold an easy origami Cat. If you’re just
starting out we suggest you take a minute to review our complete Full Tutorial on
Youtube which will teach you all the basic techniques.

Traditional Origami Cat Face Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1

Start with the white side of the paper facing up. Fold it diagonally.

Step 2

Fold the left and right corners towards the bottom corner along the dotted lines.

Step 3

Valley fold the top flaps along the dotted lines to create the cat’s ears.

Step 4

Fold the top corner down along the dotted line.

Step 5

Turn the model over, then fold the bottom layer up along the dotted line.

Step 6

Fold the tip of the top layer down along the dotted line to create the cat’s nose.
