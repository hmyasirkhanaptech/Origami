How to Fold an Easy Origami Cow

These instructions will show you how to fold an easy origami Cow. If youâ€™re just
starting out we suggest you take a minute to review our complete Full Tutorial on
Youtube which will teach you all the basic techniques.

Step 1

Fold the paper in half and then unfold it.

Step 2

Fold the top and bottom of the paper towards the centre along the dotted lines.

Step 3

Fold the top layers of paper out along the dotted lines.

Step 4

Fold and unfold all the corners along the dotted lines.

Step 5

Fold the paper along the dotted lines and make Swivel Folds along the previous creases.

Step 6

Fold the side of the paper in along the dotted line.

Step 7

Fold the paper in along the dotted lines.

Step 8

Make a Pleat Fold along the dotted lines.

Step 9

Fold the paper in along the dotted lines.

Step 10

Fold the model in half.

Step 11

Make an Inside Reverse Fold along the dotted line.

Step 12

Make a small cut along the dotted line.

Step 13

Fold up the cut paper to make ears.
