Traditional Origami Fractal Rose Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1

Fold and unfold in half both ways.

Step 2

Fold the sides to the center line.

Step 3

Fold the corners to the center again.

Step 4

Fold the top layer of each flap outwards.

Step 5

Fold out one layer to the intersection point.

Step 6

Fold out the final layer to the intersection point.

Step 7

Mountain fold the corners back.

Step 8

Curl all the corners (petals) downwards.
